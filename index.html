About Us

Meru Creation LLP, established in 1989, has been a pioneer in providing a seamless knitwear buying experience to its customers. With over three decades of expertise, we have honed our skills to offer a comprehensive range of knitwear at economic prices while maintaining superior quality standards.

Over the years, we have served esteemed clients, including global brands like Walmart and Tesco, and domestic retail giants such as Reliance and Vishal Megamart. Our approach focuses on both backward and forward integration to enhance customer satisfaction continually.
