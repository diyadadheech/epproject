About Us

Meru Creation LLP, established in 1989, is a prominent player in the knitwear manufacturing industry, known for its commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. With over three decades of expertise, the company offers a diverse range of knitwear, catering to both domestic and international markets.

Officially registered as an LLP in India, Meru Creation LLP complies with all required regulations under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs.
